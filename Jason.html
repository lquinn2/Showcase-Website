Greenways Loop

The project I am displaying in the showcase is The Greenways Loop Signage and website. I designed new wayfinding signs for the Greenways Loop, a cycling and pedestrian trail that surrounds the city of Campbell River. In addition to the signs I also created a website specifically for the trail that will showcase an interactive map of the trail that visitors can use to learn more about the trail.
